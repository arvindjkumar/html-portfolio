The Best Movies According to AJ

My top 3 Hindi movies of all-time.

Sholay

A movie about two criminals who overcome their past and help people in need.

Laila Majnu

A movie about two star crossed lovers who give up their life to be with one another when their parents do not approve.

Gadar

A movie about a sikh truck driver who crosses the border to bring back his love.

Amar Akbar Anthony

A movie about three brothers separated as children and brought up in 3 different religions. They unite and seek revenge from the villian for past rimes against their father.
